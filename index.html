PAN ‚Äì Personal Area Network

Your Own Digital Bubble!

  • Range: 10 meters (Within arm's reach)
  • Purpose: Connecting personal devices
  • Key Technology: Bluetooth, USB, Infrared

Examples:

  • Wireless mouse to laptop
  • Smartphone to wireless earbuds
  • Smartwatch syncing with phone
  • Wireless keyboard connection
  • Phone to fitness tracker

Characteristics:

  • Low power consumption
  • Short-range communication
  • Easy to set up and use
  • Perfect for personal use

LAN ‚Äì Local Area Network

The Home & Office Hero

  • Range: Single building or campus
  • Speed: High speed & low latency
  • Ownership: Single organization
  • Key Technology: Ethernet, Wi-Fi Routers

Examples:

  • Home Wi-Fi network
  • School computer lab
  • Office network with shared resources
  • Library network
  • Coffee shop Wi-Fi

Benefits:

  • Resource sharing (printers, files)
  • Centralized security
  • High-speed data transfer
  • Cost-effective for small areas

MAN ‚Äì Metropolitan Area Network

The City-Wide Web

  • Range: Entire city or large campus (50-60 km)
  • Purpose: Connects multiple LANs together
  • Operation: ISPs or city authorities
  • Technology: Microwave, Fiber-Optic

Examples:

  • City-wide broadband networks
  • Cable TV network infrastructure
  • ISP connections across a city
  • University with multiple campuses
  • Smart city infrastructure

Applications:

  • Government network systems
  • Emergency services coordination
  • Educational networks
  • Business park connectivity

WAN ‚Äì Wide Area Network

The Global Giant

  • Range: Country, continent, or entire world
  • Purpose: Connects LANs and MANs globally
  • Infrastructure: Leased lines, satellites
  • Largest Example: The Internet

Key Points:

  • Slower than LANs due to distances
  • Most expensive to implement
  • Uses routers and switches
  • Can be public or private

Examples:

  • The Internet (largest WAN)
  • Banking networks
  • Multinational corporation networks
  • Research and education networks
  • Military communications
